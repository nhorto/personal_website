# Bluesky Analytics - Detailed Feature Breakdown

What exactly would creators want to track on Bluesky? Here's a comprehensive breakdown with specific examples and why each metric matters.

---

## TIER 1: CORE ANALYTICS (MVP - Must Have)

These are the basic metrics every creator wants to know. Without these, you don't have a product.

### 1. **Follower Growth Tracking**

**What it shows:**
- Daily/weekly/monthly follower count over time
- Growth rate (% change)
- Net new followers (gained - lost)
- Follower milestones (hit 1K, 5K, 10K, etc.)

**Example Dashboard:**
```
Followers Today: 5,234 (+47 from yesterday)
7-Day Growth: +312 followers (+6.3%)
30-Day Growth: +1,205 followers (+29.8%)

Graph: Line chart showing follower count over last 90 days
```

**Why it matters:**
- Creators need to know if they're growing or stagnant
- Validates that their content strategy is working
- Gives dopamine hit when seeing growth

**Bluesky-specific angle:**
- Show follower growth compared to other Bluesky creators in your niche
- Track "exodus waves" (when big accounts join from Twitter, follower growth spikes)

---

### 2. **Post Performance Analytics**

**What it shows:**
- For each post: views, likes, reposts, replies, quotes
- Engagement rate (engagements / impressions)
- Best performing posts of the week/month
- Worst performing posts (so you know what to avoid)
- Average engagement per post

**Example Dashboard:**
```
Your top post this week:
"Just shipped a new feature that..."
- 45,230 impressions
- 892 likes (1.97% rate)
- 156 reposts
- 67 replies
- Total engagement: 1,115 (2.47%)

Your average post:
- 3,400 impressions
- 87 likes (2.56% rate)
- 12 reposts
- 8 replies
```

**Why it matters:**
- Creators want to know what content works
- Helps them double down on successful content types
- Shows what topics resonate with their audience

**Bluesky-specific angle:**
- Track performance in custom feeds (which feeds amplified your post?)
- Thread performance (how far did people read into your thread?)
- Quote post analysis (what are people saying when they quote you?)

---

- Peak engagement times (when your audience is most active)

**What it shows:**
- Your top 10 posts of all time
